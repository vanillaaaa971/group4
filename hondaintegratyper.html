Description:

Precision-engineered sport compact known for its rev-happy engine and sharp handling.

Information:

The DC5 Type R (only available in Japan) has a standard K20A 2.0-liter DOHC i-VTEC engine, producing 220 hp (164 kW). The "i" in i-VTEC stands for intelligent VTEC, using VTC (Variable Timing Control) to advance timing up to 50 degrees.
