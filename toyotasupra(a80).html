Legendary sports car known for its powerful engines and sleek design.

Information:

The 2JZ-GTE engine is famously associated with the Toyota Supra A80, one of the most iconic sports cars of the 1990s. The A80, also known as the Mk4 Supra, was produced from 1993 to 2002. It featured the 2JZ-GTE engine paired with either a 6-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission, delivering impressive performance and acceleration. The Supra A80 gained legendary status for its robust engineering, sleek design, and its starring role in the "Fast and Furious" film franchise, cementing its reputation as a high-performance icon in automotive history.
