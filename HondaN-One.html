Information:

The Honda N-One is a retro-styled kei car made by Honda for the Japanese market. Previewed at the 2011 Tokyo Motor Show, it went on sale on November 1, 2012.

Description:

The Honda N-One is a compact and stylish kei car known for its retro design and modern features. Despite its small size, it offers a spacious interior and efficient performance, making it perfect for city driving.
