Information:

The carburetted version of this engine produced 38 PS (28 kW) at 5,300 rpm and 5.5 kg⋅m (54 N⋅m; 40 lb⋅ft) of torque at 4,500 rpm. The Street could reach a top speed of 115 km/h (71 mph), while the four-wheel drive version only could attain 105 km/h (65 mph).

Description:

The Honda Acty is a small and practical utility vehicle manufactured by Honda. It's designed to be compact and efficient, making it perfect for city driving and light-duty tasks. With its small size, the Acty is easy to maneuver and park in tight spaces. It's commonly used for deliveries, small businesses, and as a versatile work vehicle
