Description:

Rotary-powered sports car revered for its agility and distinctive styling.

Information:

The Mazda RX-7 (FD3S) is a renowned sports car produced by Mazda from 1992 to 2002. Powered by a twin-turbocharged rotary engine, it's celebrated for its lightweight design, exceptional handling, and iconic styling. With its unique rotary engine sound and performance capabilities, it's a sought-after collector's car.
