Information:

The Toyota Cresta is a mid-size luxury sedan manufactured by Toyota in Japan from 1980 to 2001. Positioned above the Toyota Mark II and below the Toyota Crown, the Cresta was known for its elegant design, comfortable interior, and smooth driving experience. With various engine options and trim levels available, the Cresta catered to a wide range of customers seeking a combination of performance and luxury.
