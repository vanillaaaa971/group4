Description:

Pocket rocket hatchback celebrated for its turbocharged engine and agile handling.

Information:

Pulsar GTI-R, also known as Sunny GTI-R, was developed as top performance model of the fourth generation Pulsar N14 to run in World Rally Championship (WRC) under "Group A" regulations and was rolled out in 1990. Mechanisms featured 2000cc turbo engine that generated 230ps and 4WD controlled by "ATTESA" system.
