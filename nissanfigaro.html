Description:

The Nissan Figaro is a retro-styled compact car cherished for its vintage-inspired design, compact size, and charming character.

Information:

The Nissan Figaro, produced in 1991, is a distinctive two-door convertible with a fixed-profile design and 2+2 seating arrangement. Originally sold exclusively through Nissan Cherry Stores, it shares the front-engine, front-wheel-drive setup of the Nissan March/Micra. Despite a production run of just one year and limited to right-hand drive, its popularity soared, especially through grey imports to Britain and Ireland.
