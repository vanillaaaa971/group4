The Nissan 180SX, also known as the RPS13, is a sporty and agile compact car produced by Nissan. It's famous for its sleek design and rear-wheel-drive layout, making it a favorite among drift enthusiasts. With its lightweight chassis and powerful engine options, the 180SX offers exhilarating performance and precise handling. Originally introduced in the late 1980s, it has since become a cult classic in the automotive world, cherished for its combination of style and performance.
