Information:

Though it looks like an unassuming hatchback from the outside, under the Mirage Cyborg's hood lies Mitsubishi's 1.6-liter MIVEC (Mitsubishi's name for variable valve timing) engine, capable of an astounding 173 horsepower.

Description:

The Mitsubishi Mirage Cyborg is a sporty version of the Mirage hatchback, featuring enhanced performance and styling cues. It offers a blend of economy and excitement, making it a popular choice for budget-conscious drivers seeking a bit of fun on the road.
