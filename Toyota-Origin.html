Information:

The Toyota Origin is a stylish and elegant sedan produced by Toyota. It's designed with a classic aesthetic, blending modern features with traditional charm. The Origin offers a comfortable and refined driving experience, making it suitable for both daily commuting and special occasions. With its attention to detail and luxurious interior, the Origin embodies sophistication and class

Description:

The Toyota Origin is a special mid-size car crafted by Toyota, released in Japan from 2000 to 2001. It hit the market in November 2000, originally planned for just 1,000 units, but 1,073 were made. Sold at Toyota Store, Toyopet Store, and Corolla Store dealerships in Japan, it offered a unique ownership experience during its limited run.
