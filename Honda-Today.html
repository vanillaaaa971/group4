Description:

The Honda Today is a kei car manufactured by Honda from 1985 to 1998. Designed for urban driving, it features a compact size and efficient engine, making it ideal for navigating crowded city streets. The Today was known for its practicality and reliability, offering a convenient transportation solution for daily commuting needs.
