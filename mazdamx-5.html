Description:

Lightweight convertible cherished for its nimble handling and pure driving experience.

Information:

The Mazda MX-5 (NB) is the second generation of the MX-5 series, produced from 1998 to 2005. It continued the legacy of the MX-5, further refining its design and performance. Like its predecessor, the NB model drew inspiration from classic British sports cars and remained true to the roadster heritage.
