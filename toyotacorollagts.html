Description:

The Toyota Corolla GT-S/Sprinter Trueno (AE82) are compact sports coupes praised for their performance-oriented design, nimble handling, and reliability.

Information:

The Toyota Sprinter Trueno and its twin, the Toyota Corolla Levin, were compact sports cars produced by Toyota from 1972 to 2000. Exclusive to Toyota Auto Store and Corolla Store locations in Japan respectively, the Trueno's name means "thunder" in Spanish, while "Levin" in Old English means "lightning."
