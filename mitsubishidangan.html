The Mitsubishi Dangan ZZ is a sporty car made by Mitsubishi Motors. It was built from 1962 to 2011. The car features a sleek design and was known for its performance. It came with various engine options, including turbocharged ones. The Dangan ZZ was popular among car enthusiasts for its agility and speed.

Description:

: The Mitsubishi Dangan ZZ was a small car made by Mitsubishi Motors for Japan from 1962 to 2011. It started with Shin Mitsubishi Heavy-Industries before merging into Mitsubishi Motors. It was sold at a chain called Galant Shop. Eventually, it was replaced by the Mitsubishi eK and Mitsubishi i in 2007 and 2011, respectively.
