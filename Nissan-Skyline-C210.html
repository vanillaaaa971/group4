Information:

The fifth generation Skyline C210 was released in 1977. Unlike the fourth generation C110 "Skyline for Ken and Mary" with laid-back form, C210 had a sharp styling that was reminiscent of the third generation C10 "Ai-no-Skyline (Skyline Of Love)".

Description:

The Nissan Skyline CS210 is a compact car produced by Nissan, known for its sporty performance and sleek design. It features a stylish exterior and offers a comfortable interior with modern amenities. The CS210 model of the Skyline lineup is appreciated for its nimble handling and efficient engines, making it a popular choice for driving enthusiasts. Whether cruising city streets or hitting the open road, the Nissan Skyline CS210 delivers an exhilarating driving experience.
