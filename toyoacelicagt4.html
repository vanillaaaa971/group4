Description

Rally-bred coupe admired for its turbocharged power and all-wheel-drive traction.

Information

The Toyota Celica GT-Four, produced from 1986 to 1999, was a turbocharged AWD variant developed for rallying, known as a "homologation special vehicle." With three generations—the ST165, ST185, and ST205—it marked a significant milestone in WRC history as the first Japanese AWD turbocharged car to win titles.
