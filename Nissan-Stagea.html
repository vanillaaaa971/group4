Information:

The Nissan Stagea is a station wagon made by Nissan from 1996 to 2007. Originally created to compete with Subaru Legacy Touring wagon in Japan, it was sold exclusively at Nissan Prince Store dealerships. Sharing many parts with Nissan Skyline and Nissan Laurel, there are four versions: WC34 Series 1 (1996-1997), WC34 Series 1.5 (1997-1998), WC34 Series 2 (1998-2001), and M35 Series (2001-2007).

Description:

The Nissan Stagea is a versatile and spacious station wagon produced by Nissan. Known for its combination of practicality and performance, the Stagea offers ample room for passengers and cargo while delivering a comfortable and engaging driving experience. It's available in various trim levels, including some high-performance versions, making it a popular choice for families and enthusiasts alike. With its blend of versatility and capability, the Stagea is a versatile option for those seeking a practical yet dynamic vehicle.
