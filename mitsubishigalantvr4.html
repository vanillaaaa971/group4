Description:

The Mitsubishi Galant VR-4 is a high-performance sedan celebrated for its turbocharged engines, all-wheel-drive system, and sporty design.

Information:

In October 1987, Mitsubishi Motors introduced the new Galant, featuring a high-performance version known as the Galant VR-4. This model boasted a 2.0-liter DOHC turbo engine, along with a full-time 4WD system and 4WS (four-wheel steering), garnering significant attention.
