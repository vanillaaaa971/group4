Information:

The Corolla E90, introduced in 1987, was Toyota's sixth-generation Corolla. It was the last to be classified as a subcompact and the first to offer only front-wheel or all-wheel drive. Trim levels included Base, XL, GL, SE, and SE Limited, with a high-performance GT-i model. Production continued until 1992 for export markets, with some models sold as the Geo Prizm and Holden Nova.

Description:

The Toyota Corolla E90, manufactured from 1987 to 1991, is a compact car known for reliability and practicality. It comes in sedan, coupe, hatchback, and wagon styles, with efficient engines providing a good mix of performance and fuel economy. The interior is spacious and functional, making it a favorite among drivers worldwide for its affordability, low maintenance, and durable construction.
