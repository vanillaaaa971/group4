Description:

The Honda Beat is a compact, mid-engine convertible kei car celebrated for its open-air driving experience and nimble handling.

Information:

The Honda Beat, produced from 1991 to 1996, is a kei car featuring a rear mid-engine, rear-wheel-drive layout. It was one of Soichiro Honda's final approvals before his passing, with around 33,600 units made. Designed by Pininfarina, it capitalized on Japan's tax-efficient kei car class.
