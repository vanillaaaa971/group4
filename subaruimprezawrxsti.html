Description:

Rally-inspired sedan admired for its turbocharged power and championship-winning heritage.

Information:

The Subaru Impreza is a compact car made by Subaru since 1992, replacing the Leone. Now in its sixth generation, it comes in various body styles, including a hatchback introduced in 2008. It features flat-four engines ranging from 1.5 to 2.5 liters, with turbocharged WRX and WRX STI models. Some markets use the WRX name for these high-performance variants. The Impreza is known for its all-wheel drive, with some markets exclusively selling this configuration. It's often called Scooby colloquially.
