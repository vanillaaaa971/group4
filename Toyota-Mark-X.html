Information:

The Toyota Mark X is a mid-size car made by Toyota from 2004 to 2019, mainly for the Japanese market. It was sold exclusively at Toyopet Store locations in Japan. The Mark X succeeded the Mark II, known as the Corona Mark II and Cressida in North America. It's pronounced "Mark Ex" and combines the sporty Chaser and luxurious Cresta into one vehicle. Engine options varied for different tax rates in Japan. It was also sold as the Reiz in China and briefly in Indonesia.

Description:

The Toyota Mark X is a sleek and stylish sedan produced by Toyota, primarily for the Japanese domestic market. Known for its blend of performance and luxury, the Mark X offers a comfortable ride and sporty driving experience. It features a range of powerful engine options and advanced technology, making it a popular choice among enthusiasts. With its elegant design and impressive performance, the Toyota Mark X provides a premium driving experience for those seeking refinement and sophistication.
