Description:

The Toyota Altezza, also known as the Lexus IS300, is a compact luxury sedan admired for its upscale features, refined ride, and sporty performance.

Information:

The Lexus IS, introduced in 1998, is a compact executive car from Toyota's luxury division. Originally known as the Toyota Altezza in Japan, it was rebranded as the IS for global markets. Across its generations, the IS lineup has offered various engine options and body styles, emphasizing sportiness and intelligence.
