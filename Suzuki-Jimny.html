Information:

The Jimny 5-Door, a new SUV from Suzuki comes in 2 variants. The top variant of Jimny 5-Door is powered by the GLX AT a 1462 cc, 4 cylinder Gasoline engine that fires 101 hp of power and 130 Nm torque. The 4 seater. For added safety are provided central locking & power door locks.

Description:

The Suzuki Jimny is a compact and rugged off-road vehicle known for its nimble handling and excellent off-road capabilities. With its boxy design and lightweight construction, the Jimny is highly maneuverable and capable of tackling rough terrain with ease. It offers a practical and reliable option for adventurers and outdoor enthusiasts who seek a versatile vehicle for exploring off the beaten path.
