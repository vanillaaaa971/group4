Description:

The Subaru SVX is a grand tourer coupe known for its distinctive design, smooth ride, and advanced features like its unique window-within-a-window design.

Information:

The Subaru Alcyone SVX, known as the Subaru SVX outside Japan, is a two-door coupe produced by Subaru from 1991 to 1996. It marked Subaru's debut in the luxury/performance market and is famous for its unique 'window-within-a-window' design. The name "Alcyone" comes from the brightest star in the Pleiades constellation, represented in Subaru's logo, while "SVX" stands for "Subaru Vehicle X".
