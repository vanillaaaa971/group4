Information:

The Toyota Starlet, a subcompact car, was produced from 1973 to 1999, succeeding the Publica. It offered high-performance variants across three generations. Briefly sold in North America from 1981 to 1984, it was later replaced by models like the Vitz and Echo.

Description:

The Toyota Starlet is a compact hatchback cherished for its reliability and fuel efficiency. It's a practical and economical choice for urban driving, offering a comfortable ride and ample interior space despite its small size.
