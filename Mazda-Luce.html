Information:

On August 20, 1966, Mazda introduced a flagship model known as the Luce. The body style, characterized by an "A line" connecting front, center and rear pillars, was based on a Bertone original design from Italy, which was modified by Mazda's designers to suit the company's tastes.

Description:

The Mazda Luce is a mid-size luxury car manufactured by Mazda. Introduced in 1966, it has gone through multiple generations, offering upscale features and styling. The Luce was sold in various markets globally, showcasing Mazda's commitment to luxury and refinement.
