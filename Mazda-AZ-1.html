Information:

The Mazda AZ-1 was introduced in 1992. The AZ-1 is a mid-engined Kei car with gullwing doors and glass roof panels on top of the doors. The engine is a turbocharged 0.65-liter F6A inline-3 producing 63 hp and 63 lb/ft of torque, mated to a 5-speed manual transmission.

Description:

The Mazda AZ-1 is a small and quirky kei car produced by Mazda in collaboration with Suzuki. Known for its distinctive gull-wing doors and compact size, the AZ-1 offers a unique driving experience. Despite its small stature, it packs a punch with its turbocharged engine, making it a fun and agile car to drive. With its futuristic design and lightweight construction, the Mazda AZ-1 stands out as a rare and iconic vehicle in the automotive world.
