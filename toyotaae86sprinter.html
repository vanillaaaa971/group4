Description:

Lightweight coupe celebrated for its nimble handling and drift-friendly characteristics.

Information:

The AE86 is the fourth generation of the Sprinter Trueno. It is also known as the Toyota Levin or the Corolla GT. The name AE86 offers information on the model: A designates the engine used (type "4A"), E symbolizes the range (Corolla), 8 provides information on the generation and 6 highlights the model's declination.
