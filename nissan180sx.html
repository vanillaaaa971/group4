Description:

The Nissan 180SX (S13) is a rear-wheel-drive sports coupe renowned for its sleek design and drift-friendly characteristics.

Information:

The Nissan 180SX, produced from 1988 to 1998, is a fastback car based on the S13 chassis from the Nissan S platform. Initially equipped with the CA18 engine in Japan, it later switched to the SR20 engine. Outside Japan, it was known as the 200SX and in the US as the Nissan 240SX, powered by the KA24E and KA24DE engines.
