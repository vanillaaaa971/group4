The Alto Works RS/Z (HA21S) is powered by a turbocharged 0.7-liter K6A DOHC inline-3 cylinder producing 63 hp and 76 lb/ft of torque, while Alto Works Turbo ie/s models (HA11S) are powered by a turbocharged 0.7-liter F6A DOHC inline-3 cylinder producing 63 hp and 72 lb/ft of torque

Description:

The Suzuki Alto Works is a compact, high-performance car produced by Suzuki. With its small size and turbocharged engine, the Alto Works delivers impressive agility and speed. It's designed for enthusiasts seeking a thrilling driving experience in an affordable package. The Alto Works retains the practicality and fuel efficiency of its base model while offering enhanced performance capabilities, making it a popular choice among car enthusiasts
